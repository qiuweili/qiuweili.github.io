Bio

Qiuwei Li is a Senior Algorithm Engineer in the Decision Intelligence Lab, Damo Academy, Alibaba Group US since Nov 2021. Before that, he was a Hedrick Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics, UCLA from Summer 2020 to Oct 2021. In UCLA, he was working with Prof. Wotao Yin, Prof. Stanley Osher, and Prof. Jianwei (John) Miao. Prior to that, he was a postdoc working with Prof. Gongguo Tang and Prof. Michael B. Wakin at Colorado School of Mines from Summer 2019 to Summer 2020. He received his PhD in Electrical Engineering from Colorado School of Mines under the supervision of Prof. Gongguo Tang in Summer 2019.

Research Interests

  • Understand the optimization landscapes and foundations of deep learning and nonconvex problems.

  • Develop guaranteed optimization methods that can efficiently navigate the optimization landscapes.
